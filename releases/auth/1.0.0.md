# @damatjs/provider-auth 1.0.0

> Damat ships a strict auth service contract, not a vendor adapter or identity
> engine.

## Added

- `AuthProviderService(config)`, extending the generic provider service with
  mandatory session and API-key operations.
- Transport-neutral normalized credentials, principal types, and structural
  startup validation.
- Principal lookup plus API-key issue, safe inspection/listing, verification,
  revocation, and optional rotation in the auth contract.
- Secret-free `ApiKeyRecord` and one-time-secret `IssuedApiKey` result types.
- Concrete normal-module lifecycle fixtures and backend startup/HTTP coverage.
- Nested normalized header and cookie records redact during serialization, not
  only the outer credentials wrapper.

## Removed before 1.0

Vendor factories and the separate unpublished `@damatjs/api-key` package were
removed. Better Auth, Clerk, and Auth0 adapters remain retired at their prior
published versions. Provider-specific sign-in, OAuth, recovery, and management
routes belong to the installed module.
